Hi! I'm Gus, a 16 year old student at Queen Elizabeth High School, and this is my icebreaker project.

To begin, I was born January 8th, 2003, in Santa Monica. As I was born in the U.S.A, and moved to Canada when I was 3, I'm a dual citizen. I enjoying reading, playing video games, watching plays & musicals, geeky things in general (I've been going to Calgary Comic-Con every year since I was 5), and in warmer weather I spend a lot of time longboarding or biking, this past summer I recieved my black belt in Taekwon-Do. One of my interests that transitioned from a job to a hobby is acting. My first ever time acting was when I was around 7 years old, in the documentary series Pet Heroes, which reenacted real life events of people's pets saving their lives. Since then I've had numerous voice acting roles, along with multiple on stage performances and on-screen roles. I pride on my (as described by outside sources) witty sense of humor, and creative thinking.
